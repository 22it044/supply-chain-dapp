How Product Tracking Works

Each product in our supply chain has a unique identifier that is recorded on the blockchain. When you enter a product ID:

  1. The system verifies the product's existence on the blockchain
  2. All supply chain events related to the product are retrieved
  3. You can see the complete journey of the product from production to sale

Product IDs can typically be found on product packaging or purchase receipts.
